I was born in April 1994 in the Argentine province of Mendoza.
I have a large family between brothers and nephews, which I love very much.
My city is known as the cradle of good wine;
we have varied landscapes and our greatest strength is tourism.

What I love the most in my life is the small family that I have formed with my two children and my partner.
I really like animals, especially dogs, their loyalty and companionship is unique.

My partner and father of my children are programmed with systems and I was always curious about the subject,
but I was never encouraged, until I found Khan Academy and fell in love with his beautiful way of educating.
And from now on I want to continue internalizing myself in the world of programming
